About Kuala Lumpur

ASEAN's financial hub

Kuala Lumpur is fast emerging as ASEAN's launchpad for financial innovation — a dynamic hub where next-generation services, sustainability, and regional integration converge.

Malaysia's economy is powering cross-border flows, digital asset growth, and intelligent infrastructure amid ASEAN's push toward a $2 trillion digital economy.

Positioning the Summit here reflects both where growth is happening and where policy, infrastructure, and capital are converging in 2026.
